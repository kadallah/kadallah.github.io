Supernova

Supernova is a multimedia live performance encapsulating a one-act play the inner struggles of a creative mind -- the impact of creativity and imagination on the psyche. The performers are an artist contemplating suicide and his inner geist, providing inspiration on the topic of immortality. The use of motion capture technology translates the live performance of the flesh-and-blood actors to virtual space -- as an extension of the stage and as provision for a vivid interpretation of a perturbed mind.

The piece was created using Unreal Engine and the Optitrack Motion Capture Studio. I wrote the piece and performed it with Michelle Hessel in December 2016.
