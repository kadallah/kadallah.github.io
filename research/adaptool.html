Adaptool

Conceptual
Prosthetic Limb

The current prosthetic market is stuck on the idea of recreating the hand. But what if there was an alternative that not only cut out the middle man but augmented the abilities of amputees so they were even more efficient at completing tasks than before they lost their limbs?

Adaptool goes above and beyond all current solutions for those that have lost their arms, taking advantage of technology that currently exists and putting them to use in ways previously unconsidered to help the disabled reach unthought of potential.

Adaptool was selected as the 2015 Microsoft Design Expo Most Impactful award.
